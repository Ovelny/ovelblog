chasm - a personal wiki

Chasm is a personal wiki, recently rewritten from scratch. I intend to update and maintain it with the following principles in mind:

  • Simplicity: information will be as straightforward as possible.

    • Present tense only, unless it is required to use something else.
    • Reasonable use of acronyms, and if used, always indicate their definition in the same file.
    • Frequent use of analogies for hard-to-grasp abstract concepts.

  • Conciseness: information will remain as quick to read as possible.

    • Concepts should be explained in short sentences, unless it becomes counter-productive to do so.
    • Short files to let you read and learn concepts as quickly as possible.
    • Offload longer explanations (specs, RFCs, etc) with links and references.

  • Intuitiveness: information will be found and parsed easily.

    • Simple organization: directories for domains to learn, files for each concept explained.
    • No nested directories: if files are piling up in a domain, a quick ctrl-f should be enough to do the work.

Found a mistake or a typo? Feel free to make a pull request here: https://github.com/ovelny/ovelny.github.io/
