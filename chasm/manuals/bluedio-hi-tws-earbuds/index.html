Bluedio hi tws earbuds

Charging up

The case can charge earbuds 2-3 times before needing a recharge itself.

  • Charging the earbuds: put the earbuds in the battery case and press the charging button.
  • Charging the battery case: connect the case with an USB cable and wait for the charging light to turn red.

Pairing

The earbuds support auto-pairing. When taken off the battery case, they will automatically turn on and enter pairing mode. Turn on bluetooth on your desired device and pick "Bluedio Hi TWS".

Things can get messy and the earbuds can pair to the phone before pairing between themselves: to reset auto-pairing, press the button on each earbud 3 times when powered on.

To forcefully reboot the earbuds, press both buttons and hold for 10 seconds.
