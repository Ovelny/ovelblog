Fittonia

Care

Another tropical plant that grows in the shadow, under the trees.

  • Likes moisture and very little light
  • Likes temperatures above 13°C, ideal temperature: 20°C
  • Needs constant humidity: perfect for a bathroom and similar areas
  • Keep the soil moist during spring and summer
  • Wait for a dry soil before watering during fall and winter
